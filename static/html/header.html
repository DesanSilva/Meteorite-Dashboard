Meteorite Landings Dashboard

Interactive Analysis of Meteorite Discoveries Across Time and Geography

Dataset Overview

Data Characteristics

  • Total Records: 45,716 meteorite landings
  • Geographic Coverage: Global coordinates
  • Time Period: 860 CE - 2025 CE
  • Mass Range: 0.01g - 60,000kg

Key Variables

  • Classification: 450+ meteorite types
  • Discovery Type: Fell vs Found
  • Mass Distribution: Highly right-skewed
  • Temporal Patterns: Exponential recent growth

Three Time Periods

  • Period 1 (≤1752): Historical discoveries
  • Period 2 (1753-1973): Scientific era
  • Period 3 (≥1974): Modern systematic searches

Key Insights

  • Most meteorites are ordinary chondrites
  • Antarctica is a major discovery hotspot
  • Recent decades show exponential growth
  • Technology revolutionized detection methods
Data Source: Meteorite Landings dataset from NASA's Open Data Portal
Processing: Cleaned, filtered, and categorized for interactive visualization
